About us

"We cultivate the young mind today & prepare the backbone of nation tomorrow"

"Parbat Green Peace English Boarding School is an academic institution located in Parbat district in Nepal.Established in 2066, The school is known for its excellent faculty and staff, who are dedicated to providing a high-quality education to all students. br The school has a strong emphasis on English language education, The school has modern facilities and resources to support student learning, including well-equipped classrooms, a library, computer lab, and playground. The school also provides boarding facilities for students who come from other areas. The school's campus is safe and secure, providing students with a safe and healthy environment in which to learn and grow.
The school also has a strong extracurricular activities program, which includes sports, music, and art, to help students develop their talents and interests outside of the classroom. This helps to create a well-rounded student who is able to excel academically and in their chosen pursuits. The school also provides opportunities for students to participate in community service and volunteer activities. The school is committed to providing students with a high-quality education that will prepare them for success in the future.


Message From Chairperson

Dear Parents and Guardians,
I am honored to welcome you to the Parbat Green Peace English Boarding School, established in 2066 BS. Our school is committed to providing a safe and nurturing environment for our students to learn and grow. We understand that entrusting your child's education to us is a big decision, and we take this responsibility very seriously. Our dedicated faculty and staff are committed to providing the highest quality education to our students, and we are constantly working to improve our programs and facilities. Message From Principal

Dear Students, Parents, and Guardians,
Welcome to the Parbat Green Peace English Boarding School. As the Principal, I am honored to lead this community of dedicated educators, motivated students, and supportive families. We understand the importance of education and strive to create a learning environment that fosters academic excellence and personal growth. Our experienced faculty and staff are committed to providing our students with a well-rounded education, including strong academic programs, extracurricular activities, and service opportunities. Our boarding school provides a unique and valuable experience for our students.
